Many years ago, The Corruption attacked the universe of Izlya.
Many people have been attacked by this disease and have perished.
The Lord of Corruption was dominating everyone and life in the universe.

The Guardian of Izlya was then created, one who would protect the heart of the universe.
The Guardian and the Lord of Corruption faced each other fiercely.
The Lord was banished from the universe after losing the first war.
But it cost Izlya's Guardian vitality.

Before leaving the universe, the Guardian separated his soul and powers in two, and gave life to these little born souls.
The Son of Ice, the one who would bring the deadly cold to those who disobey his order.
And the Son of Fire, the one who would destroy enemies just by walking on his feet.

Lance was born from the deadly fire, the one that takes life away from him, but lives in the shadow of loneliness.



Name: Lance Maroshek.
Nicknames: The Demon Hunter/The Crimson Rose.
Age: Unknown (he stopped aging at 23 years old).
Gender: Male.
Race: Demon.
Powers and Abilities: He can manipulate Fire and Darkness to attack his enemies, and purify the corruption in people's heart, has super fast moves, can regenerate his own injuries.

A mystical creature born from the powers of mortal fire, Lance is considered to be one of Adam's two sons, The Guardian of Izlya, after the guardian almost died in the war against the Lord of Corruption, he separated his powers and his soul in two, from these souls two creatures were born capable of manipulating the powers of a powerful entity like Adam. Quit Maroshek, the older brother, was born with the touch of deadly ice, being able to summon powerful blizzards and ice spears to eliminate what remained of corruption in Izlya, he had a calm and protective personality, always willing to help the weak, he was loved by the population of the universe.

Then Lance was born, the one with the deadly touch of fire, he was the one who could destroy an entire city with just an outbreak of rage, the one who could even wake up volcanoes. With each step Lance took, he would create a trail of fire and destruction wherever he went, his fire would drive people away from him and frighten everyone around with his destructive power, thus making the immortal creature feared and lonely in the universe.

His brother was always admired, despite bringing the cold with him, he never understood why everyone loved Quit more than he did, and Lance grew up with the need to always get the attention of his older brother, wanting to prove that he is also worthy of love. and compassion, both for people and for his own brother, who saw Lance as a sign of death in his aura.

But it was all in vain, and Lance's desperation grew together like a blaze of fire in his heart.

Lance decided that he would prove to people that he was capable of this recognition, and mainly of his brother's recognition, decided that he would face Corruption alone, but after an attack that caused a wound in his heart, Lance discovered that the mixture of Corruption with his powers he could create a flame that would purify the disease of the body of the people, thus bringing a possible cure to the whole Izlya.

Perhaps Lance would finally be able to unite again with his brother and show that together they could release the chains of corruption in the universe.

Meanwhile, the Lord of Corruption just watched Lance from a distance, planning his destruction.
